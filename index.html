Proposal & Rencana Operasional • 2 Nov 2025

Ringkasan Acara

Sifat: Non-kompetitif, tanpa BIB (fokus komunitas)

Lokasi: Heritage Lifestyle Hub (Start/Finish)

Rute 10K: HLH → Santika → Hero (WS1) → Tol Parigi (U-turn) → Aluvia → ECI (WS2) → Shell → HLH (finish)

Rute 5K: HLH → Santika → Flyover Loops (U-turn) → ECI (WS2) → Shell → HLH (finish)

Jadwal & Urutan Start

  • 04:00: Crew arrival & set-up
  • 04:30: Briefing Crew & Marshall (HT, peta, tugas)
  • 05:15: Briefing Volunteer & Water Station (WS)
  • 05:30: Briefing Pacer & MC buka area
  • 06:00: DJ performance start & Fun warm-up
  • START (Stagger 5 Menit)
    • 06:15 — 10K Pacer 6’ (BALON MERAH)
    • 06:20 — 10K Pacer 7’ (BALON KUNING)

    • 06:35 — 5K Pacer 7’ (BALON KUNING)
    • 06:40 — 5K Pacer 8’ (BALON BIRU)

Detail Pacer (24 Orang)

  • Tugas: Guiding tempo, safety, & friendly support
  • Perlengkapan: Balon tinggi ("PACER 6/7/8"), reflective tape, peluit
  • Formasi: 2 di depan + 2 di samping/rolling
  • Kumpul: 05:30–05:45 untuk briefing akhir

Penempatan Marshall (15 Orang)

  • Koordinator (Wheels): 2 orang (04:30–09:00)
  • Wheels (Start/Finish): 3 orang (05:30–09:00)
  • Hotel Santika (belok): 1 orang (05:45–08:00)
  • Hero (WS1): 1 orang (05:50–08:00)
  • Flyover Loop (U-turn 5K): 1 orang (06:10–07:10)
  • Tol Parigi (U-turn 10K): 1 orang (06:00–08:00)
  • Aluvia: 1 orang (06:00–08:00)
  • ECI (WS2 & Medis): 1 orang (05:50–08:00)
  • Shell (belok finish): 1 orang (06:00–08:00)
  • Mobile Marshall/Sweepers: 3 orang (06:00–08:30)

Operasional Rute, WS, & Medis

Operasional Rute (06:15–08:00)

Mobile patroli lapor ke koordinator tiap 15 menit. Insiden via HT, koordinator kerahkan mobile/medical.

Water Station (WS)

  • WS1 (Hero) & WS2 (ECI): Aktif 05:50–08:00
  • Perlengkapan: Air galon, dispenser, cup, tissue, trash bag

Medical & Safety

  • Pos Medis: ECI & Finish Area
  • Evakuasi: Ambulans on-call & 1 mobile medical
  • Kontak: Marshal/koordinator pegang daftar kontak emergency

Finish & Post-Event

  • 06:45–08:30: Finish handling (games, refreshment, snack)
  • 07:30–08:15: Photo spot & family zone
  • 09:00: Closing ceremony

Checklist Kunci & Perlengkapan

  • Balon Pacer: Merah=6’, Kuning=7’, Biru=8’ (tulisan "PACER X")
  • Komunikasi & ID: HT channel set & test; rompi/ID untuk semua marshal
  • Signage: Cone & petunjuk di semua belokan & titik putar balik
  • Dokumentasi: Photographer & drone (briefing no-fly zone/time). Prioritas: WS & finish line.
  • Mobile Sweepers: Motor/sepeda dilengkapi P3K kecil & HT
