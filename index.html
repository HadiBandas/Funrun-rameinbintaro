Heritage Lifestyle Hub Bintaro

1st Anniversary FUN RUN - Rencana Operasional

Tanggal: 2 November 2025

Ringkasan Acara

Acara: Heritage Lifestyle Hub Bintaro — 1st Anniversary FUN RUN

Sifat: Non-kompetitif, tanpa BIB (fokus komunitas & experience)

Lokasi: Wheels (Start/Finish)

Rute 10K: Wheels → Hotel Santika → Hero (WS1) → Tol Parigi (Putar balik) → Aluvia → ECI (WS2) → Shell → Wheels (finish)

Rute 5K: Wheels → Hotel Santika → Flyover Loops (Putar balik) → ECI (WS2) → Shell → Wheels (finish)

Jadwal & Urutan Start

  • 04:00: Crew arrival & set-up
  • 04:30: Briefing Crew & Marshall (HT, peta, tugas)
  • 05:15: Briefing Volunteer & Water Station (WS)
  • 05:30: Briefing Pacer & MC buka area (tone fun)
  • 06:00: DJ performance start & Fun warm-up (dengan instruktur)
  • START (Stagger 5 Menit)
    • 06:15 — 10K Pacer 6’ (4 pacer, BALON MERAH) - Est. Finish 07:15
    • 06:20 — 10K Pacer 7’ (4 pacer, BALON KUNING) - Est. Finish 07:30
    • 06:25 — 10K Pacer 8’ (4 pacer, BALON BIRU) - Est. Finish 07:45
    • 06:30 — 5K Pacer 6’ (4 pacer, BALON MERAH) - Est. Finish 07:00
    • 06:35 — 5K Pacer 7’ (4 pacer, BALON KUNING) - Est. Finish 07:10
    • 06:40 — 5K Pacer 8’ (4 pacer, BALON BIRU) - Est. Finish 07:20

Detail Pacer

  • Jumlah Total: 24 pacer (4 orang per pace per kategori)
  • Tugas: Guiding tempo, safety, dan friendly support (non-competitive)
  • Perlengkapan: Balon tinggi (tulisan "PACER 6/7/8"), reflective tape, peluit kecil
  • Formasi: 2 di depan + 2 di samping/rolling
  • Kumpul: 05:30–05:45 untuk briefing akhir & persiapan

Penempatan Marshall (Total 15 Orang)

  • Koordinator Marshall (Wheels): 2 orang (04:30–09:00) — HT utama & eskalasi
  • Wheels (Start/Finish): 3 orang (05:30–09:00)
  • Hotel Santika (belok): 1 orang (05:45–08:00)
  • Hero (WS1): 1 orang (05:50–08:00) — WS & dokumentasi
  • Flyover Loop (putar balik 5K): 1 orang (06:10–07:10)
  • Tol Parigi (putar balik 10K): 1 orang (06:00–08:00)
  • Aluvia: 1 orang (06:00–08:00)
  • ECI (WS2): 1 orang (05:50–08:00) — WS kedua & pos medis
  • Shell (pembelokan ke finish): 1 orang (06:00–08:00)
  • Mobile Marshall / Sweepers: 3 orang (06:00–08:30) — Patroli, P3K, backup, HT

Operasional Rute, WS, & Medis

Operasional Rute (06:15–08:00)

Mobile patroli melapor ke koordinator setiap 15 menit. Jika ada insiden, marshal titik lapor, koordinator kerahkan mobile/medical.

Water Station (WS)

  • WS1 (Hero) & WS2 (ECI): Aktif 05:50–08:00
  • Perlengkapan: Air galon, dispenser, cup, tissue, trash bag

Medical & Safety

  • Pos Medis: ECI & Finish Area
  • Evakuasi: Ambulans on-call & 1 mobile medical siap evakuasi
  • Kontak: Marshal/koordinator pegang daftar kontak emergency

Finish & Post-Event

  • 06:45–08:30: Finish handling (games,refreshment, snack)
  • 07:30–08:15: Photo spot & family zone
  • 09:00: Closing ceremony

Checklist Kunci & Perlengkapan

  • Balon Pacer: Merah=6’, Kuning=7’, Biru=8’ (tulisan besar “PACER X”)
  • Komunikasi & ID: HT channel set & test; rompi/ID untuk semua marshal
  • Signage: Cone & signage petunjuk di semua belokan & titik putar balik
  • Dokumentasi: Photographer & drone (briefing no-fly zone / time window). Prioritas foto: WS & finish line.
  • Mobile Sweepers: Motor/sepeda dilengkapi P3K kecil & HT
